Quality & Safety Commitment

We follow a zero-compromise policy for safety and work quality

Quality Assurance Practices

  • Detailed project planning before site mobilization
  • Approved material submittals and vendor evaluations
  • Method statements and work procedures for each activity
  • Stage-wise inspection and documentation

Safety Protocols Include

  • Mandatory PPE for all workforce
  • Regular toolbox and safety training sessions
  • Site risk assessments and emergency preparedness plan
  • Compliance with KSA construction safety standards
